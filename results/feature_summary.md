# Feature Summary

## Feature Groups
- Log-mel spectrogram
- MFCC
- Delta MFCC
- Delta-delta MFCC
- Spectral centroid
- Spectral bandwidth
- Spectral rolloff
- Zero crossing rate
- RMS energy
- Spectral flux

## Pooling Strategy
Each feature is summarized using:
- Mean
- Standard deviation
- Median
- 10th percentile
- 90th percentile

## Motivation
Combining cepstral and spectral features improves robustness and captures both frequency and temporal information.
